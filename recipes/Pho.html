Pho is one of the most well known Vietnamese foods found across all areas of the world. A stable in Vietnam, this soup, consisting of both, rice noodles, herbs, meats (usually beef), or meat alternatives such as tofu or mushrooms, this dish has been perfected by the Vietnamese since the early 20th century Unfortunately due to how poorly documented pho is, there is generally disagreement over the cultural influences that led pho to being a national dish in Vietnam.

Despite the varying styles, what can be agreed upon, is that pho is absolutely delicious.
